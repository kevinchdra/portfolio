Renjana: The Eternal Resonance
With Indonesia Film Festival Team
Motion Graphics, Identity
Published in 2023

Established in 2006, the Indonesian Film Festival (IFF) is Australia’s largest student-led celebration of Indonesian cinema, dedicated to fostering cultural appreciation and raising international awareness of the nation’s growing film industry. Held annually in Melbourne, the festival showcases a diverse range of films — from cult classics to contemporary thrillers — and continues to serve as a platform for Indonesian storytelling abroad.

In 2024, the 18th edition carried the theme “Renjana: The Eternal Resonance”, exploring the preservation of cinematic heritage and the connection between past and present. I contributed custom motion title work showcased at ACMI, alongside visual identity development, promotional content, and merchandise design.

The festival welcomed over 600 attendees and featured appearances by leading figures in Indonesian cinema, including Asmara Abigail, Ardhito Pramono, and Ibrahim Risyad.
