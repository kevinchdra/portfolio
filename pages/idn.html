InDonation Festival
With PPIA RMIT & @indonation.au
Visual Merchandising, Identity, Motion Graphics
Published in 2023

Launched in 2010, InDonation is a student-led charity festival run by PPIA RMIT, dedicated to supporting underprivileged communities and underrepresented causes across Indonesia. Each year, the festival brings together music, art, and community in a vibrant celebration of youth-led change.

In 2023, InDonation shifted its focus to animal welfare. We contributed to the festival’s evolving visual identity by introducing two playful mascots — Meng the cat and Mong the dog — designed to spark empathy and engagement for the cause. As part of the fundraising effort, we designed a line of merchandise which became incredibly successful and a key contribution towards the campaign.
